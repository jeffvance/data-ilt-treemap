Relational Databases

For many years, relational databases have been commonly used by applications developers and ubiquitously supported by IT organizations - essentially treated as a defacto standard. Relational databases underpin legacy applications that meet current business needs; they are supported by an extensive ecosystem of tools; and there is a large pool of labor qualified to implement and maintain these systems.

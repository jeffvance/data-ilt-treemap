Applied Data Scient Project

Red Hat has an internal "Applied Data Scient Team" (operated our of our "Emerging Technology" team). Evaluating / scoring "popularity" for various "data" technolgies, is a good exmple of an applying data analytics, to unstructured data, to derive insight. Red Hat will (likely) do a project to quantitative score all offerings in all categories (of the unified taxonomy we are using). Rationale for Red Hat to do this project:
  • Gives us complete "coverage" of the overall data landscape (not just database subset)
  • Control: we can weight sources however we want, etc.
  • Can (hopefully) highlight strengths of storage offerings
  • Makes an excellent demo of how to use best in class tools with RHT products to solve a real-world (important) problem When?
